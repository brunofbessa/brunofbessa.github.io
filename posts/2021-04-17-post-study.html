Article Review: "On Power-Law Relationships of the Internet Topology", By Faloutsos et al.

A study on the evolutive topology of the Internet

Posted by Bruno F. Bessa on April 17, 2021

Purpose

This 1999 article aims to study the time evolution of the internet topology. It also discusses the benefits from understanding the topology of the internet.

Background and Previous Work

The internet can be studies at two different granulatities: router level (each router is a node in the network) and inter-domain level (each domain represented by a single node and each edge is an inter-domain connection).
